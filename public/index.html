Common Questions

What should I bring to my first visit?

Please bring your ID, insurance card, and any previous dental records or X-rays you may have.

Do you offer emergency dental care?

Yes, we prioritize dental emergencies. Please call us immediately or use our Telegram bot for urgent scheduling.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, UPI via our online booking system, and cash payments at the front desk.
